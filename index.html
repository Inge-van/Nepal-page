I traveled to Nepal and loved every minute of it

In september 2018 I started my solotravel. The trip would take 8 months in total and during the first two I traveled through Nepal. Very early into my trip I had met two other travelers and together with a guide we went trekking in the Mount Everest region. It was 16days trekking in the mountains, it was the best experience of my life. With a lot of ups and downs, literally, came the most amezing views.
